Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common investor questions

How do I start investing with AfriInvest?

To start investing, first create an account and complete our investor verification process. Once approved, you can browse available opportunities, review project details, and make investments through our secure platform.

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment varies by project but typically starts at $500. Some projects may have higher minimums based on their funding requirements.

How are investment opportunities vetted?

All projects undergo rigorous due diligence including financial analysis, market research, legal review, and on-site evaluations by our investment team before being listed on our platform.

What fees does AfriInvest charge?

We charge a 2% platform fee on invested capital and a 10% performance fee on profits. There are no hidden fees, and all charges are clearly disclosed before you invest.
